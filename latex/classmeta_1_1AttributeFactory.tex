\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Factory for runtime creation of attributes by type name. 

This registry enables deserialization and dynamic instantiation of attributes without compile-\/time knowledge of their concrete types.

It maps string identifiers to factory functions producing \doxylink{classmeta_1_1AbstractAttribute}{Abstract\+Attribute} instances.
